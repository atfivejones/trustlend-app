Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first contract?

Click "Create Note" in the navigation, fill in the loan details and party information, then proceed through our guided signing process.

Are these contracts legally enforceable?

Yes! Our contracts are UCC Article 3-104 compliant and designed to hold up in small claims court. Users have a 100% success rate so far.

What's the difference between the $9.99 and $19.99 plans?

The $19.99 Maximum Protection includes blockchain verification, ID verification, court evidence packages, and priority support. The $9.99 Essential Protection covers basic legal contracts with digital signatures.

Can I get a refund if my contract doesn't work?

Yes, we offer a money-back guarantee. If your TrustLend contract doesn't help you recover your loan, we'll refund your fee.

How do payment reminders work?

We automatically send professional email and SMS reminders to borrowers before due dates and after missed payments, so you don't have to chase people for money.
