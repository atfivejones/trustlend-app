üí° Pro Tip: Keep It Simple

Avoid Interest: Charging interest to family/friends can complicate relationships and may require additional legal compliance.

üèõÔ∏è Small Claims Court Process

1

Send Demand Letter

Required in most states. Give 10-30 days for response. Use TrustLend's demand letter generator.

2

File Court Papers

Visit local courthouse or file online. Filing fee usually $30-$100. Bring your contract and evidence.

3

Serve the Defendant

Court will serve papers or you can hire a process server. Cost usually $50-$150.

4

Attend Court Hearing

Present your case to the judge. Bring all documents, evidence, and witnesses if available.

5

Collect Judgment

If you win, you may need to take additional steps to collect (wage garnishment, asset seizure).
